About Me

The past several years I have been in and out of college trying to figure out what I wanted to do. Back in 2014, I decided to enroll in college for Fall semester. My major was Business Administration. While searching for electives, I came across a Web Development class. Curiosity propelled me to enroll. Before taking the Web Development class, I had never really given any thought about websites or how to create one, let alone programming. I'd never even heard of programming. After taking the class. I started teaching myself more HTML5 and CSS3. I ended up taking another class, which started my path into Web Development.

I have learned HTML5 CSS3, and Responsive design. I am learning Git/GitHub and JavaScript to further my Front End development skillset.

I am working to become a Full Stack Developer, so I will be learning some backend languages. Though becoming a Full Stack Developer is my current goal, it is not my final goal. I am just beginning on this path. I look forward to reaching my goals and going wherever they take me.
