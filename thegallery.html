This is The Gallery. The Gallery is a mock website. It was made just for practice. The gallery is a responsive website made using HTML5, CSS3, and some JavaScript. I used Adobe XD for wireframing and to try out different layouts.

While this is just a simple, one-page website I wanted to do something different. Rather that put the website title in the center of the header, I decided to put it at the bottom of the header. The header container is also fixed on larger screens so that the navigation, which shows alongside the title on larger screens, so that it will be easily accessed.
